My Name is Jeremy England and I live in Garland, TX.

My hobbies include:
- Music
- Rocket League
- Making my Kids Laugh
- Talking about Life
- Politics

I am married with two kids.

Currently I am serving as an Executive Pastor at [[Journey Community Church]] in East Dallas. 

I am also struggling through a Doctorate of Ministry through Nazarene Theological Seminary in a spiritual formation and discipleship focus, specifically trying to help the church understand clergy members who are [[neurodivergent]]. This is of significant importance to me as I was diagnosed with [[ADHD]] as an adult. 

I studied and earned a Master of Ministry Degree from [[Mount Vernon Nazarene University]] and completed my education requirements towards ordination at Nazarene Bible College. 

My collegiate experience started by by studying music education at [[Bowling Green State University]] (go Falcons!). I taught music for 9 years before switching full time into ministry.

I am a veteran of Operation Iraqi Freedom (2008-2009) and Operation Enduring Freedom (2012) after completing deployments with the Ohio Army National Guard. 

I am on here to document and learn in a public environment, which is a form of body doubling and accountability from you to me (Thanks!).
