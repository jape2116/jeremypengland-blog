It is really easy to start the process of addressing disability concerns when there is relatively low investment by the local congregation. However, it often does not go far enough because there are many facets of disabilities that are overlooked by able bodied people making these decisions. Addressing all areas of a disability is a testament to how we treat all people, because [[Atomic Reading Notes/treating-disabled-people-gives-insight-to-our-theology]].
