## Questions from Amish in regards to Adopting a New Technology

• How does this technology change what it means to be human (for better/worse)?
• How does this technology change what it means to live in a community (for better/worse)?
• How does this technology change human/earth/other animal relations (for better/worse)?


We can't change everything, but we can make small little moves to improve the destruction we bring upon the world when interacting with it.
